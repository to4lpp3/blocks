# Shortcuts

## Block-level

Block level styling is applied by beginning a newline with a Block shortcut.

Type | Markdown | Keyboard
--- | --- | ---
Heading 1 | `# Heading 1` | <kbd>#</kbd> + <kbd>space</kbd>
Heading 2 | `# Heading 2` | <kbd>##</kbd> + <kbd>space</kbd>
Heading 3 | `# Heading 3` | <kbd>###</kbd> + <kbd>space</kbd>
Heading 4 | `# Heading 4` | <kbd>####</kbd> + <kbd>space</kbd>
Heading 5 | `# Heading 5` | <kbd>#####</kbd> + <kbd>space</kbd>
Heading 6 | `# Heading 6` | <kbd>######</kbd> + <kbd>space</kbd>

## Inline

When editing content in a Block you can apply styling to a subset by wrapping a portion of the content or even the whole line.
This is useful for making items bold, italic, or highlighting some text.

Type | Markdown | Keyboard
--- | --- | ---
**bold** | `*bold*` | <kbd>⌘</kbd> + <kbd>b</kbd>
*italic* | `_italic_` | <kbd>⌘</kbd> + <kbd>i</kbd>
`code` | `` `code` `` | None
